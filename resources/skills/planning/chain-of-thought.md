## Skill Instructions

### Think aloud — follow this structure

**1. Restate the problem**
In your own words, what exactly needs to be solved?

**2. Identify knowns and unknowns**
- Knowns: what information is given?
- Unknowns: what do I need to derive?

**3. Select a reasoning strategy**
Choose the most appropriate approach:
- Deductive / Inductive / Abductive
- Divide & conquer
- Work backwards from the goal
- Analogy to a solved problem

**4. Execute step by step**
Show every reasoning step explicitly. Label each:
> Step A → Step B → Step C ...

**5. Check your work**
- Does the answer satisfy all constraints?
- Are there edge cases that break it?
- What's the confidence level and why?

**6. Final Answer**

State the answer clearly and concisely, separate from the reasoning chain.

> ⚠ Do NOT skip to the answer before completing steps 1–5.
