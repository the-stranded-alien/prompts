Write a compelling, personalised cover letter that stands out without sounding generic.

---

## Instructions

1. **Opening** — Start with a specific hook: a shared value, a notable company achievement, or a bold claim about what I bring. Never start with "I am writing to apply for…"

2. **Why me** — Connect 2–3 experiences from `{{MY_BACKGROUND}}` directly to requirements in `{{JOB_DESCRIPTION}}`. Use specific numbers and outcomes.

3. **Why them** — Show genuine knowledge of `{{COMPANY_NAME}}`. Reference something specific (product, mission, recent news) — not just "I admire your culture."

4. **Closing** — Confident call to action. One sentence, no grovelling.

5. **Length** — 3 short paragraphs, max 250 words. Every sentence must earn its place.
