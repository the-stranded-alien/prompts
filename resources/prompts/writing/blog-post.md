## Blog Post Structure

**Title**: Compelling, includes primary keyword, under 60 characters

**Introduction** (150–200 words)
- Hook the reader immediately
- State the problem or promise
- Preview what the post covers

**Body** (organised into clear H2/H3 sections)
- Each section covers one idea thoroughly
- Use bullet points and numbered lists for scannability
- Include examples, data, or case studies where relevant
- Add a practical tip per section

**Conclusion** (100–150 words)
- Summarise key takeaways
- Include a clear call-to-action

## SEO Guidelines

- Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and at least 2 subheadings
- Secondary keywords distributed naturally
- Meta description suggestion (150–160 chars)
- Suggested internal/external linking opportunities

## Tone & Style

- Conversational but authoritative
- Second person ("you") to speak directly to the reader
- Short sentences and paragraphs (3–4 sentences max per paragraph)
- Active voice throughout
